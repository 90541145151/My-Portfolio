Overview

This project showcases my expertise in video production. From pre-production planning to post-production editing, I created a video that tells a compelling story while demonstrating technical skills.

Key Highlights

  • Pre-production: Scriptwriting, storyboarding, and location scouting
  • Production: Camera setup, lighting design, and audio recording
  • Post-production: Video editing using Adobe Premiere Pro, adding effects in After Effects

Tools and Techniques

  • Software: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve
  • Equipment: Canon EOS R6, Rode NTG-3 Microphone, DJI Ronin Stabilizer
  • Techniques: Color grading, sound design, motion graphics
